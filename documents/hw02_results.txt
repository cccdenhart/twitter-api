Without broadcasting:

  Post rate: 35,714 tweets / second
  Timeline retrieval rate: .05 retrievals / second

With broadcasting:

  Post rate: 1,672 tweets / second
  Timeline retrieval rate: 1,063 timelines / second

Results:

  Although the results are as predicted, they are even more contrasting than I might have guessed.
  Without broadcasting, posting rate far surpassed relational database rates.  Retrievals, however,
  were even worse than before. Broadcasting showed more promising results.  While post rates simply
  matched relational levels, timeline retrievals well exceeded relational rates.
